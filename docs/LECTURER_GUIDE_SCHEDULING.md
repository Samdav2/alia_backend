# Lecturer Guide: Scheduling Course Content

You can now schedule when specific modules and topics become available to your students. This allows you to plan your curriculum in advance and release content progressively.

## How to Schedule Content

When creating or editing a **Module** or **Topic**, you will find an optional field called `Available At`.

1. **Set Date and Time**: Provide the date and time (UTC) when you want the content to be released.
2. **Immediate Availability**: Leave the field empty or set it to the past if you want the content to be available immediately upon publishing the course.
3. **Saving**: Click Save to apply the schedule.

## What Students See

- **Locked Content**: Before the scheduled time, students will see the title of the module or topic with a "Locked" indicator and the release date.
- **Hidden Content**:
    - For **Modules**: All topics within a locked module are hidden from students.
    - For **Topics**: The main text content, video URLs, and other media files are hidden until the release time.
- **Automatic Release**: Content becomes available automatically once the scheduled time is reached. Students may need to refresh the page to see the newly available content.

## Tips for Success

- **Consistency**: Try to release content at a consistent time (e.g., every Monday at 9:00 AM).
- **Grace Periods**: Consider scheduling content a few minutes before a live session to ensure all students have access when you start.
- **Staff Bypass**: Remember that you and other administrators always see the full content, regardless of the schedule. Use "Student View" (if available) to verify your scheduling.
