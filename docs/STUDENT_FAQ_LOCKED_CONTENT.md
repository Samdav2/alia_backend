# Student FAQ: Locked Course Content

Some parts of your course may be "Locked" for a specific period. This is because your instructor has scheduled the content to be released at a later time.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Why is this module/topic locked?
Instructors often schedule content to be released progressively to keep the class on track or to prepare for specific sessions.

### When will the content be available?
You will see the scheduled release date and time next to the module or topic title. Once that time is reached, the content will automatically become available.

### Do I need to refresh the page?
Yes, you may need to refresh your browser page to see the newly unlocked content once the scheduled time has passed.

### I see the module but not the topics inside it. Why?
If a module is locked, all topics within it are also hidden to provide a clean overview of the course structure.

### I have access to the topic, but it says "Locked until scheduled time". What gives?
The topic has been released, but the specific details (like video or reading material) are scheduled to be revealed at the specified time. This is common for quizzes or pre-reading materials.
